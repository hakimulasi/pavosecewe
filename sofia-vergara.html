Sofia Vergara News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Name:Sofia Vergara

Hometown:Barranquilla, Colombia

Relationship:Separated

Birthday:July 10, 1972

About

Sofia Vergara is best known for playing Gloria Delgado-Pritchett on ABC’s Modern Family from 2009 to 2020, a role that earned her several Emmy and Golden Globe nominations. Vergara also starred in 2011’s New Year’s Eve, 2014’s Chef and 2015’s Hot Pursuit before returning to TV in the 2024 Netflix crime drama Griselda, which she also executive produced.

The actress began starring as a judge on America’s Got Talent in 2020 alongside Simon Cowell, Howie Mandel and Heidi Klum. Vergara shares son Manolo with ex-husband Joe Gonzalez and was briefly engaged to actor Nick Loeb. She married Joe Manganiello in 2015, but the pair announced their split in 2023.
