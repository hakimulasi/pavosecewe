What nationality is Donna Hanover?

Publish date: 2024-08-14

Donna Hanover is an American journalist, radio and television personality and actress who served as First Lady of New York City from 1994 to 2001. Donna Hanover holds a Master in Journalism degree from the Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism.

Donna Hanover was born in Oakland, California, to Robert G. Kofnovec and Gwendolyn Dolores Domas on February 15, 1950, hence, she is 72 years of age.
