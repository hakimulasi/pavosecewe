Toto Vega (born September 23, 1969, age: 52 years) was a famous media personality, actor, and artist from Columbia. He made his name in Hollywood and across the world for his performances in different movies, stage shows, and TV shows.

Some of his notable characters in TV shows like Mala Yerba, Operation Pacific, Bolivar, The Secret Law, and many others have made him a star in the film industry. He has organized many film festivals for movie lovers. Toto remained active in the industry from 1992 to 2022 before his death. He died on 25th September 2022.

What was the Reason for Toto Vega’s Death?

Actor Toto Vega died while organizing and managing the celebration of the Green Film Festival in Barichara, Santander. For this festival, he was performing as a founder and director. He faced an acute myocardial infarction or we can say a heart attack when he was performing his duties as a founder and director of the festival on 25th September 2022. Then he was immediately shifted to a hospital in the municipality of San Gill where he was declared dead.
