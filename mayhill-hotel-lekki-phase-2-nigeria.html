Mayhill Hotel, Lekki phase 2, Nigeria

Publish date: 2024-08-09

A stylish boutique hotel in Lekki Phase 2, Lagos

Situated in a serene part of townMayhill Hotel is located in 12, Muritalu Eletu Street, Osapa London, Lekki Phase 2. Business, leisure, couples and transit travelers can book a stay in the finely polished suites. Free WiFi is available.

The hotel feature rooms, spaciously designed, fit with state of the art amenities and a private bathroom.

The hotel has an outdoor swimming pool for relaxation and recreation. Conference and banquet facilities are available at a cost.

Luggage storage and laundry services are available. Car hire services are also available on request. The premises is guarded by security personnel and on-site parking is available. Mayhill Hotel is a 10 minute drive from The Palms Mall, Lekki
