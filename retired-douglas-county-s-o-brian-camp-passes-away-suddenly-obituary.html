Retired Douglas County S.O. Brian Camp passes away suddenly: obituary

Publish date: 2024-09-04

Douglasville, GA – The unexpected passing of Brian Camp on Friday, October 27, 2023, has left a deep impact on the hearts of those who knew him. His sudden departure has prompted an outpouring of condolences and tributes from the community, expressing profound sadness over his loss.

Originally from Winston, Georgia, Brian Camp spent his formative years there before settling in Douglasville, where he resided until his passing. He leaves behind his wife, Ashlie Hope Camp, who is in need of love and support during this trying time.

Brian Camp’s professional career spanned over thirty years, culminating in his retirement as a Lieutenant from the Department. In the late 2010s, he notably contributed his expertise to the Training Division.

Apart from his dedicated service, Brian was also recognized for his musical talents. “Brian Camp and the Georgia River Band” was a locally renowned musical ensemble known across Northwest Georgia, showcasing Brian’s skills as a singer and songwriter. Additionally, he had a passion for fishing that was well-known among those who knew him.

The circumstances surrounding Brian Camp’s passing, including the precise cause of death, remain undisclosed at this time. Reports suggest a possible heart attack while he was engaged in woodcutting on his property, although specifics have yet to be confirmed.

As the community mourns this tragic loss, heartfelt condolences continue to pour in for Brian Camp’s family, urging support and compassion during this difficult period.

Information regarding Brian Camp’s obituary and funeral arrangements will be communicated by his family, providing closure and an opportunity for the community to honor his memory.
