Obsessed Fans That Spent Thousands To Look Like A Celebrity

Publish date: 2024-07-21

At this point, we've established that obsessed fans getting plastic surgery to look more like their celebrity idols is a thing, but in this case, a celeb's own relative may have gone under the knife to fine-tune a resemblance. 

According to InTouch, actress Lindsay Lohan's estranged half-sister, Ashley Horn, spent $25,000 to look like Lohan. "I've gotten rhinoplasty, a bit of refinement underneath my cheeks and jawline, some fat injected into my chin and some fat injected into my upper cheeks," Horn supposedly said, adding that the results made her look "hotter than Lindsay."

However, in 2013, Horn appeared on a talk show to dispel rumors that she had surgery to try to resemble Lohan. She even passed a lie detector test and delivered a personal message to the Mean Girls star: "I am so sorry for letting the media use me to drag you into this."
