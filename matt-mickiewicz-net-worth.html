What is Matt Mickiewicz's Net Worth?

Matt Mickiewicz is an internet entrepreneur who has a net worth of $100 million dollars. Born June 27, 1983, Matt Mickiewicz is best known as the co-founder of SitePoint, an Australian-based website and publisher of courses, books and articles for web developers. He also co-founded the web companies Flippa, an online marketplace for buying and selling websites, domains and iOS apps, also based in Australia, and 99designs, an online graphic design marketplace headquartered in San Francisco, California with offices in Melbourne, Berlin, Paris, London and Rio de Janeiro. In 2012, 99designs acquired the European creative services marketplace 12designer and the following year the company set up European headquarters in Berlin, also launching localized versions of its services in German, French, Spanish, Dutch and Italian.

The company also acquired the Brazilian design marketplace LogoChef that same year. In 2010, Mickiewicz' company won the Webby People's Voice Award for Best Web Service and Application. Mickiewicz started his first business at age 14, filling a void he felt for a directory of web-development resource tools and resources, as he wanted to learn to build websites as a hobby.
