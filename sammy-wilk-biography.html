Who is Sammy Wilk?

Sammy Wilk is an American singer, songwriter, and entrepreneur.

He has released singles like Higher, Waterfall, Way Up, Issues, and more. He is mainly related to Reggae fusion and pop genres.

Sammy Wilk: Earl Life, Education

He was born Samuel Howard Wilkinson on Dec 11, 1995. His birthplace is Omaha, Nebraska.

His father is Dave Wilkinson and his mother is Lori Wilkinson. As for siblings, he has a brother Benny and two sisters, Annie and Emily. He holds Caucasian ethnicity.

Talking about education, Sammy Wilk attended a state school.

Sammy Wilk: Professional Life, Career

Sammy Wilk in his early career used to upload videos on Vine. His first single music video was released in July 2015 titled Aye Ma.

Then he came up with his debut album Ready For War in 2016. His other singles drooped in 2017 named Doesn’t Make Sense, Let It Go, Way Up, and Public Figure.

He has also teamed up with Karina Rae for Best Mistake, Skate for Nothing To A King, and Jack & Jack for Throw Signs.
