Jill Duggar, Derick Dillard's Walmart Baby Registry Revealed

Publish date: 2024-07-06

Whatever Baby Dilly wants, Baby Dilly gets! 19 Kids and Counting star Jill Duggar and her husband, Derick Dillard, still have several months to go before welcoming their firstborn son, but they've already got a pretty good head start on shopping for the future bundle of joy. The couple, who married in July, registered for baby gifts at their local Walmart in Rogers, Ark., where Dillard is a tax accountant. 

Like their wedding registry — which included requests for cereal, instant noodles, WD-40, neon duct tape, and even guns — the Dillards' baby registry features some surprising (but very practical) items. Among them? Orange Gatorade, Fanta, Dr. Pepper, a fitness stabililty ball, and gift cards to Olive Garden and Subway.

Of course, the first-time parents-to-be also registered for a bevy of baby things, such as diapers, wipes, bibs, baby oil, pacifiers, blankets, changing pads, car seats, and bedding. Bigger-ticket items include a Fisher-Price Jumperoo, a Graco "Newborn Napper Station," and a jogging stroller.
