Taylor Swift Shows Off Legs in Catalina With Lorde, Jaime King: Photos

Publish date: 2024-08-02

Taylor Swift and her famous pals know how to have fun. The "Blank Space" singer hung out with Lorde, Jaime King, and Olympic figure skater Gracie Gold on Catalina Island in Southern California on Friday, Jan. 9.

"Wandering around Catalina with my favorite people to wander around with," Swift, 25, captioned an Instagram photo of the foursome. The ladies were also joined by members of the band Haim, sisters Este, Danielle and Alana Haim.

The group appeared to be doing some shopping, presumably turning heads with their eye-catching fashion. Swift showed off her long stems in a cranberry dress and donned a brown floppy hat. Lorde wore dark jeans and a top with "Lonely" written on it, while the Hart of Dixie actress buttoned up in an olive-colored coat. Gold fit right in with an all-black ensemble and a light pink scarf.

The afternoon outing, however, wasn't just a BFF outing. King's husband Kyle Newman and even Swift's father walked close behind while admiring the scenery.

"Surround yourself with those that make your Soul flourish," King captioned the same shot. In a second pic, Swift quipped: "Scott Swift, the newest member of @haimtheband."
