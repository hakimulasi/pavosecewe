Diddy Remembers Kim Porter While On Vacation Check Out His Sweet Tribute

Publish date: 2024-09-03

Earlier today, the artist took to his social media to post a clip of a beautiful sunset he caught on camera while vacationing in Italy. Diddy took this opportunity to dedicate the gorgeous footage to none other than his late ex, Kim Porter.

He captioned the story vid with a sweet sentiment to the mother of his daughters.

The 49-year-old musician seemed to be thinking about Kim during that time and paid a sweet tribute to her as a result.

Diddy simply tagged Kim on Instagram and added a red heart emoji over the clip.

The model passed away shockingly and suddenly back in November and ever since, the rapper has been remembering her a lot on social media, even saying he came to regret not marrying her before.

The video he shot from a boat also features Zion Train by Bob Marley in the background and Diddy can also be heard saying ‘I see you, baby’ referring to Kim.

But that was not the man’s only post from this vacation as he also shared a few other vids featuring unforgettable moments with family, including his and Kim’s twin daughters, D’Lila Star and Jessie James, 13.

There was also one of Diddy watching a Bob Marley performance on TV.

In the meantime, rumors have been going around that he and 22 year old Lori Harvey are dating.

As it turns out, they have been longtime friends and she’s even helping him cope with Kim’s passing.

One source tells HollywoodLife that ‘Lori has been friends with the whole family for years. But she and Diddy’ve gotten a lot closer over the past 6 months or so, since Kim’s death because Lori was really close to Kim too. She was grieving as well, so she was able to be there for Diddy in his grief. She uplifts him.’
