Robert Pattinson, FKA Twigs Are Engaged After Six Months of Dating

Publish date: 2024-08-12

The hearts of Twilight fans are breaking everywhere! After just six months of dating, Us Weekly can confirm that Robert Pattinson and FKA twigs are engaged.

As previously reported, the couple exchanged promise rings with each other last month. But a source now tells Us that the singer's new jewelry is actually an engagement ring. (T-Pain first revealed the pair are engaged during an interview with Vulture, but later said it was an April Fools' Day joke.)

"I hear they were telling people they were promise rings, but I haven't seen Pattinson wearing one. They only told a few people about the engagement," the insider tells Us. 

"They've been inseparable. He's planning on going to Coachella to support her," the source adds. "They've been living together and he's been calling her his greatest love."
