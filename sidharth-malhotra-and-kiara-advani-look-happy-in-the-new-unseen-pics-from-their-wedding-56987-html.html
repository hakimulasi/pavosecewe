Sidharth Malhotra and Kiara Advani look happy in the new unseen pics from their wedding

Publish date: 2024-08-29
Sidharth Malhotra and Kiara Advani tied the knot on February 7 in Suryagarh Palace in Jaisalmer, Rajasthan. The couple took to their social media handle and shared their dreamy wedding pictures with a caption, "Ab humari permanent booking hogayi hai.” Many photos from their wedding went viral online and sparked a frenzy among their fans.

Now, a fresh batch of previously unreleased images has recently appeared on social media, showing Sidharth Malhotra and Kiara Advani cheerfully posing for a photo with their guests while dressed in their wedding ceremony attire.


In the picture, the newlyweds are seen posing with their guests along with fashion designer Manish Malhotra. Kiara is wearing her stunning pink ombre bridal lehenga with beautiful embroidery all over it. Sidharth Malhotra, on the other hand, looks dapper in his golden sherwani. Fashion designer Manish Malhotra who is also standing beside the couple wore a navy blue kurta with white pyjamas and a pink shawl.
Additionally, the photos showcase the exquisite decor, which included stunning crystal chandeliers hanging from the ceiling, and the multifoil arches in the background gave them a regal feel.

Meanwhile, on Tuesday, Sidharth Malhotra and Kiara Advani shared numerous pictures from their Mehendi ceremony where Kiara and Sidharth coordinated in white and yellow attires.
