Chris Distefano Biography

Christopher Paul Anthony Distefano is an American comedian who is known for his appearance on MTV and MTV2’s shows Guy Code and Girl Code. He has also done long standup specials, Chris Distefano: Size 38 Waist and Speshy Weshy. Currently, Paul is co-hosting the podcast called Hey Babe! along with Impractical Jokers’ Sal Vulcano and hosts his own podcast, Chrissy Chaos.

Distefano was born in Brooklyn, New York, and raised in the Ridgewood neighborhood of Queens and attended Archbishop Molloy High School. After high school, he enrolled at St. Joseph’s College where he played basketball and studied psychology. In addition, he furthered his study at the New York Institute of Technology where he graduated with a doctorate in physical therapy. Shortly after graduation, he began his professional career as a full comedy time. Moreover, he also hosted Fencing Masters on the SNY network & Elite 8 as well as participated in Madness Comedy Competition.

Aside from that, Christopher also took part in the headline Carolines Breakout Artist Series as well as appeared in the finalist Comedy Festival. He also performed standup at venues including New York Comedy Club, Comedy Cellar, Gotham Comedy Club, and Broadway Comedy Club among others. In 2012, Distefano was cast in MTV’s Guy Code, which was in its second season as well as starred in the show Girl Code.

On the other hand, he has also appeared in other shows such as Guy Code vs. Girl Code, Off the Bat, The Challenge, and Guy Court. Later after, he released his half-hour special at Comedy Centra called The Half Hour. Besides that, Chris also appeared on Late Night with Seth Meyer’s show as well as toured in the U.S. along with Monster Energy Outbreak Presents. In 2015, he co-starred in the IFC series Benders with Andrew Schulz and starred in the Netflix show called Ultimate Beastmaster.

Additionally, Distefano also co-starred in the web series Bay Ridge Boys alongside fellow comic Yannis Pappas. Later on, he launched his podcast titled History Hyenas and also did a standup special Chris Distefano: Size 38 Waist premiered on Comedy Central. In 2022, Distefano sold out his first performance at New York City’s Beacon Theatre. After that, he recorded his second special, Speshy Weshy at the Gramercy Theatre and thereafter hosted an episode on MLB’s Off The Bat and MSG’s The Brackets. Most recently, he was featured on The Pat McAfee Show podcast, hosted by the athlete and sports commentator Pat McAfee.
