Ebanie Bridges plays 'f***, marry, kill' with Conor McGregor, Anthony Joshua and Jaron Ennis

Publish date: 2024-08-14

Ebanie Bridges admitted she would sleep with Conor McGregor in a game of 'f***, marry, kill'.

However, Anthony Joshua might have to watch his back.

That is despite an awkward photo that emerged of McGregor and Bridges at Katie Taylor's bout against Chantelle Cameron earlier this month.

During a Q&A session with fans, Bridges was happy to spill the beans when asked some personal questions.

One fan asked who would she rather 'f***, marry or kill' out of McGregor, Joshua and American boxer Jaron Ennis.

Bridges laughed and said: “Hahaha god ya’ll are killing me, what did I get myself into?"

She went on to admit that she would "f McGregor" as he "seems like fun".

Ennis is the lucky groom as Bridges opted to marry the 25-year-old, who she called "sweet and quiet".

And that left Joshua as the kill, "because I've never met him," Bridges added. "Lol soz mate."

Unfortunately for Bridges, McGregor is already married and has been with Dee Devlin since they were teenagers.

The two recently met in Dublin as they watched Taylor lose to Cameron by majority decision.

But Bridges was still in a buoyant mood and took to Instagram to post a picture of her with McGregor.

One supporter responded by saying: "He looks like he's manhandling you in every picture."

Bridges jovially replied: "Felt like he was not going to lie lol."

The 36-year-old is the current IBF bantamweight champion after beating her old foe Shannon O'Connell in December.

The contest was settled by technical knockout in the eighth round at the First Direct Arena, Leeds.

Bridges is a huge Leeds fan and recently joked that she would hand out her OnlyFans for free if they stayed in the Premier League.

But Sam Allardyce could not work his magic as the Whites were relegated on the final day of the league season.

Leeds lost 4-1 at home to Tottenham but Everton's win against Bournemouth meant they would have gone down anyway.
