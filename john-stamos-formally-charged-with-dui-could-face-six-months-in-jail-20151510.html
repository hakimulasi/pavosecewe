John Stamos Formally Charged With DUI, Could Face Six Months in Jail

Publish date: 2024-07-06

Have mercy. John Stamos has formally been charged with DUI stemming from his June arrest, Us Weekly can confirm.

The Full House actor, 52, faces one misdemeanor count and a possible six months in jail, the Los Angeles County District Attorney's Office told Us on Thursday, Oct. 15. Stamos' arraignment is scheduled at 8:30 a.m. on Friday.

As previously reported, the Grandfathered star was pulled over on June 12 while driving in Santa Monica, Calif. It was reported that he was taken to the hospital "due to a possible medical condition" at the time. The following month, he checked himself into rehab for substance abuse for 30 days.
