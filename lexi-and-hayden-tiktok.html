Lexi And Hayden Tiktok The Couple Received Hate

Publish date: 2024-07-23

Lexi And Hayden Tiktok’s video has been circulating on the internet for a while. As much as people have adored them, they have received immense hate from the online audience.

Lexi and Hayden are the latest couple whom TikTok users adore to despise. These two, who posts under the name @lexi and Hayden, have a devoted following because of their unusual way of living. 

They share a camper in Oklahoma and have recently started working different jobs at night. 

Hayden works until 5 a.m., and Lexi works until 7 a.m., as she explained in this video. They typically go to bed when she gets home from work and doesn’t wake up until 2:30 or 3 p.m.

Other interesting details about the couple involve Hayden playing video games frequently and Lexi enjoying singing.

They also have an Alex, their shared dog. Although it is unknown when they were born, Lexi stated that she was 19.

She also wished Hayden a happy 21st birthday in September.

The duo has more than 137,500 followers but also some detractors. On Reddit, disparaging discussions about the pair have already begun to increase. 

There is now an entire community called r/Lexi and HaydenSnark Lounge.

Lexi made movies about “Hayden’s sibling” evicting them or something similar before they moved into one’s travel trailer.

Lexi and Hayden on TikTok received hate

According to the r/Lexi and HaydenSnark Lounge, most of the hostility appears to be directed at Lexi, the account’s main face.

Lexi is despised because her stories frequently contradict one another, and she begs, stating that “they can’t afford groceries or laundry, but then goes out and buys a $200 gaming monitor,” according to a commenter.

Someone in another thread noted that Lexi was sporting an Apple watch despite claiming to be having financial difficulties.

More purported reasons why people weren’t too fond of the couple, especially Lexi, were provided by one user in a later post. 

“They keep expired food in their refrigerator and cook uncooked meat on the stove. They simply don’t clean up after themselves, despite the fact that she calls herself a “housewife,” and yet she gives him a pb&j for lunch,” the user entered.

Additionally, users criticized Lexi for allegedly lying a lot in her videos.

Someone wrote: “It’s just a collection of odd, little lies. That raises the question, “What is the purpose of that? “Why lie?” 

She once asserted, for instance, that she was an only child, a granddaughter, and no cousins.

Then she claimed to have a single cousin, which is strange.

According to other users, she allegedly makes up the details of cleaning the camper and attending class.

Things became nasty when Lexi allegedly started circulating false information online regarding Hayden’s cousin Hayley Wertman.

Hayley and Lexi seemed to engage in physical contact. Additionally, a Reddit user going by the handle u/Pine apple-bra and claiming to be Hayley posted the following in the group: “If any of you would like some tea. Tonight, Lexi attacked me for challenging her!”

They don’t get along, despite the fact that what happened between them is unclear.
