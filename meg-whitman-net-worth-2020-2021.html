Early life:

Meg Whitman was born in Cold Spring Harbor, New York, on 4th August 1956 to Margaret Cushing and Hendriks Hallett Whitman. She attended Cold Springs Harbor School from where she graduated in the year 1974. Initially, a science student, she switched over to economics in which she did her B.A in the year 1977 and thereafter obtained an M.B.A from Harvard Business School in 1979.

Career

Meg Whitman started her career as a Manager in Proctor and Gamble Company in Cincinnati, Ohio, and thereafter, she worked as a consultant at Bain and Company San Francisco, where she rose to the rank of Vice President.

She joined Walt Disney Company in 1989 as Vice President, and after this, her next assignment was at Stride Rite Corporation in which she became President and CEO. She was also General Manager at Hasbro Playschool Division.

Meg Whitman joined eBay in 1998, and in the year 2008, the company had a phenomenal growth of annual revenue from 4 million dollars to 8 billion dollars. She made many changes in the business model of the company and splitting it into 23 categories and assigning each category to different executives. Her ten years on eBay was the most fruitful and innovative period of her career.

She joined as CEO of Hewlett Packard in September 2011 and left in 2017.

Meg Whitman endorsed Mitt Romney’s Presidential campaign in 2008 and later John McCain’s campaign, who lost to Barack Obama. In 2016 she supported Chris Christie’s Presidential campaign, who withdrew and endorsed President Donald Trump. Meg Whitman thereafter endorsed Hillary Clinton’s presidential campaign.
