Early Life

Ne-Yo was born on 18th October 1979 in Camden, Arkansas, with the name Shaffer Chimere Smith. Both of his parents were musicians. His mother, who was his only guardian as she separated from his father, motivated him a lot. His first R&B group was Envy. Producer Big D Evans gave him his nickname Ne-Yo.

Age, Height, and Weight

Being born on 18 October 1979, Ne-Yo is 44 years old as of today’s date 20th July 2024. His height is 1.73 m tall, and his weight is 74 kg.

Career

His first band, Envy, did not last long and was disbanded in the year 2000. Then he signed a contract with Columbia Records. He first came into limelight with his song ‘The Girl.’ Now Ne-Yo is known as one of the most talented songwriters in America. He wrote songs for many famous artists like B2K, Teedra Moses, Christina Milian, and Faith Evans.

His debut album ‘In My Own Words’ was a big success, and it ranked no. 1 on Billboard 200. Again his next album ‘Because of You’ got huge appreciation among the fans and critics. Later he wrote songs for some other famous artists like Michael Jackson, Rihanna, Mario Vasquez, and Beyonce Knowles. Some of his other famous albums are ‘Year of the Gentleman,’ ‘Libra Scale,’ ‘R.E.D.,’ ‘Non-Fiction,’ and ‘Good Man.’

Awards & Achievements

Awards are not very tough to come for this talented singer. He received a Soul Train Music Award for Best New Artist in the year 2007. In the same year, he got the BET Award for Best Male R&B/Pop Artist, which he later got again in 2009. In 2008, he got the Grammy Award for Best Contemporary R&B Album.

2009 was the best year for him as other than BET Award for Best Male R&B/Pop Artist; he got some other awards too like Grammy Award for Best R&B Song, Grammy Award for Best Male R&B Vocal Performance, and Soul Train Music Award for Best Collaboration. Other than this, he got Billboard Music Award for Top Radio Song in the year 2012.
