Lottie Tomlinson Poses for Lipsy London in New Photoshoot

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Lottie Tomlinson may be absolute magic when it comes to helping style others – she will be working with the gorgeous Selena Gomez on her tour this year – but the blonde beauty is also just as enchanting in front of the camera with beautiful make up displayed on herself!

Lottie’s Instagram and Snapchat are full of photos, almost daily, that show off her different makeup looks.  From days relaxing at home, at shoppes, or even going out on the weekend – her looks are always gorgeous with just enough edge!  She’s a beauty inspiration for a lot of young adults everywhere, inspiring them to step outside the box and try that daring lip color or mascara trend.

On April 18th, on her Instagram page, she showed us all a sneak peek of her posing for Lipsy London!

Lottie’s hair, clothes, and make up look absolutely perfect in the shoot, which comes as no surprise, the girl is flawless!  We can’t wait to see the rest of the photos to see how she’s styled!

Lipsy London is a British fashion brand based in London that is made up of fashion lovers who aim to get cat walk looks in the closet of consumers quickly! They have something for everyone; from wedding pieces to date night and even casual outings with friends – Lipsy has you covered!

Lottie posted another look later in the afternoon and she looks fierce!  This time with a black off the shoulder top, wavy hair, and long lashes – paired with a nude lip, this look is gorgeous!
