Katie Holmes Haircut: Darker, Shorter Hair in Picture

Publish date: 2024-09-02

It's not a pixie or a bob, but Katie Holmes went darker and shorter for the holiday season.

The Romantics actress, who turns 35 this month, debuted a new, sophisticated, chocolate-brown hair color and shorter, cleaner cut at the Z100 Jingle Ball in NYC last weekend. The transformation was so subtle that fans nearly missed her new beauty look, which includes a color nearly identical to Kate Middleton's November transformation.

At first glance, the brunette's new 'do seems slight, but the face of Bobbi Brown made moves: Suri's mom cut off at least five to six inches of her hair.
