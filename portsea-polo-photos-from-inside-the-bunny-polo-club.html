Portsea Polo: Photos from inside the Bunny Polo Club

Publish date: 2024-08-05

It might not be as well known as prestigious racing events like the Melbourne Cup, but the Portsea Polo certainly knew how to draw a crowd over the weekend.

Hundreds of event-goers flocked to Point Nepean National Park, located in Victoria’s Mornington Peninsula, on Saturday – and they did not hold back in the fashion stakes.

Plunging necklines and midriff-baring outfits ruled the day, with most female polo-goers opting for either floral or white looks.

As the day wore on, polo-goers weren’t afraid to let loose at the Bunny Polo Club – or put up their feet for a well-deserved rest.

There were some famous faces in the crowd, with reality TV queen Keira Maguire rocking a black jumpsuit by Misha and wide-brimmed hat.

Also in attendance was Maguire’s ex, Bachelorette star Jarrod Woodgate.

Woodgate and Maguire began dating after meeting on the first season of Bachelor in Paradise.

However, the reality TV couple split for good mid last year after an 18 month on-off relationship.

Despite their break-up, Maguire and Woodgate seem to be on good terms and were seen chatting together at the Portsea Polo.
