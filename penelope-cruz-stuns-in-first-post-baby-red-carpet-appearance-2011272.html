Penelope Cruz Stuns in First Post-Baby Red Carpet Appearance

Publish date: 2024-09-08

Muy caliente, mama!

One month after welcoming a baby boy, Penelope Cruz dazzled on the red carpet at Sunday's Academy Awards.

Walking hand-in-hand with her Oscar-nominated husband Javier Bardem, Cruz, 36, showed off her enviable figure in a curve-hugging red L'Wren Scott design. The new mom accessorized her awards show ensemble with yellow oval and pear shaped chandelier earrings and a pink sapphire ring, both by Chopard.

It is Cruz's first red carpet appearance since she gave birth to their first child last month in L.A., reportedly named Leo Encinas.

Although the Spanish superstar couple have remained fairly mum about their new family, Bardem couldn't help but gush while accepting a Goya Award in his native Spain Feb. 13.

"I dedicate [this award] to my wife and son for awakening my heart and smile every day," Bardem, 41, said.
